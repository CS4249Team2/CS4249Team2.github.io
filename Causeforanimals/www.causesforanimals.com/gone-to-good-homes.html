GONE TO GOOD HOMES (Recent Adoptions)
DOMINO & MAGIC
Domino and Magic are finally home !
Dom and Magic were a pair of siblings who were bailed out by us from the pound in the early part of this year as a pair of juvenile cats. They had been waiting for a forever home of their own but took much longer than many others who had came after them. Perhaps they were shy and had what was deemed to be “ordinary looking.” Days and months came and went but nobody was keen on giving them a chance.
Luo min and her hubby had been looking feline pal to join them in their new home and the boys caught her attention . The first encounter wasn’t an encouraging one as boys were extremely shy and refused to open up to the couple. They were not deterred and instead came back again with toys and treats determined to win them over.
Today, Dom and Magic are their favourite cuddle and tv buddies and the two lucky boys are also pampered with loads of home made and fancy toys. Thank you for choosing to adopt and for not giving up on them ! Special thanks to Jennifer, Dani and Aisling who had fostered the boys when they were waiting for their forever home.
RUSS
Russ has found his forever home with Diane and Marc!
Russ was a street dog who was part of the TNRM Programme in the Western end of Singapore. After sterilising Russ and his pack, we gradually worked on rehoming them. Russ was well cared for by the workers in the area but they too felt a home environment would be the best for a street dog.
Marc and Diane had been looking for a canine companion for a while and Russ managed to win them over very quickly. Russ is now home! We would like to thank Marc and Diane for their patience and riding through the initial period with Russ till he gradually got better in a home environment.
Thank you for your patience, love and most importantly giving a street dog a home to call his own!
SEPHY
​Sephy has found a home to call her own!
Sephy was originally rescued off the streets with her mum. She was shy and took a while to learn to walk on leash. Sephy was also so nervous about the change in environment she took a while to get adjusted to walks and her toilet habits.
With much patience and time, Sephy blossomed. We would like to thank Pearlyn and Ryan for their patience and giving Sephy the time she needed to warm up. Sephy is now renamed Kiki and enjoys tonnes of outdoor adventures with her pawrents!
BOB
Bob was adopted as a puppy from us but later given up due to changes in circumstances of the family. Such cases really sadden us. It is what it is and we choose to look on the bright side of things!
Bob is a lucky boy. We happened to have met Kezia who was looking for a canine companion to call her own. Kezia had spent lots of time fostering Bali Street dogs previously and had lots of love and patience for a Singapore Special.
Bob now enjoys long walks with Kezia and tonnes of beach time too. We'd like to thank Kezia for opening her heart and home to Bob when the going got tough.
