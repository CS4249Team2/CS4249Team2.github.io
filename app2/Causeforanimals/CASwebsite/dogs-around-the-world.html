DOGS AROUND THE WORLD
Our adopted dogs have travelled far & wide!

Lucky and Trisha in Australia
​
Look who's made it all the way to Australia!

Lucky and Trisha were adopted by Carl and Sarah from us a few years ago. Carl and Sarah had to return to Australia this year. With the horrid Covid situation, the future seemed so uncertain. Flights were cancelled and all other services heavily restricted.

In spite of this, Carl and Sarah did not give up for Lucky and Trisha are family. They adopted the sisters as puppies. Lucky was a hit and run victim that Carl and Sarah nursed back to health. Never once was leaving the dogs behind an option. We would like to thank Carl and Sarah not only for adopting but taking the duo with them to Australia.
​
A pet is for life and we are blessed to have met Carl and Sarah who gave both Lucky and Trisha their forever home.
​
Marmaduke in Philippines
​
Say hello to Marmaduke all the way in the Philippines!

Marmaduke was rescued as a hit and run victim from Tuas. He was fostered and then adopted by the amazing Deepti! Deepti recently made the move from Singapore to the Philippines and there was no doubt in her mind that Marmaduke would go wherever she would.

Marmaduke is now enjoying the high life in the Philippines and is well loved by Deepti and her husband. We would like to thank them for giving Marmaduke a home off our streets and loving him like family. ❤️
Alvin in London

Someone's half way round the world!

Lucky Alvin, now renamed Bailey was rescued from a drain with his siblings. Bailey was adopted by Elizabeth and her family last year. When Elizabeth discovered they had to leave Singapore in a rush to go home to Heathrow, she somehow managed to pull everything together.

We love people like Elizabeth for many give excuses and have a tonne of reasons why the dog shouldn't come with them but Elizabeth knew he was family and there was no doubt he would fly with them home ❤️
Thank you for adopting Bailey and giving him a home off our streets!
Mya & Tim in London

Look who's all the way off in London!

​Mya and Tim who were both adopted from us have since left Singapore for London with Saskia and Jorge. The duo were adopted seperately as puppies and when it was time for Saskia to leave, she knew she would be bringing her two canine pals with her. Mya and Tim are two very lucky street dogs who are now living it up in London!

Thank you for adopting two Singapore Specials!
Rose in America

Rose was born to a construction site in the Western end of Singapore. She was rescued with her siblings and mother who all eventually found amazing homes to call their very own!

Rose was eventually adopted by  Margaret and her beautiful family. When it was time for them to leave Singapore and return to America, Rose was up and ready to go with them. Rose now spends her days with Margaret and family in Houston and enjoys the vast green fields and chasing after grasshoppers!
​
Thank you for adopting Rose and giving her a life of love and adventure!
Teddy in America

Teddy says Hello from America!
Teddy was found neglected, unkempt and skinny. He had not been cared for very well but yet he was still full of love and adoration for humans.
Teddy was rescued and fostered by Alice and Schmike. They helped him put on weight, get used to proper toilet habits and learn to be part of a home again. He eventually melted the hearts of Nicole and her hubby who adopted Teddy.
When they decided to leave Singapore, Teddy left with them. There were no second thoughts, buts or ifs. Teddy was family and Nicole knew she would take him wherever she went! Thank you Nicole for adopting and giving Teddy a life he could only dream of. 
Buddy in Australia

Buddy says HI! all the way from stunning Australia!

Buddy used to be street dog who roamed a factory area with his sister in the far end of Singapore. His feeder approached us to help find the duo a home to call their on as road kill was common in the area due to heavy traffic.
​
Buddy was eventually adopted by Yen Ping and her husband. When they were scheduled to leave Singapore due to a job offer, they knew Buddy would join them no matter what it would take. Buddy has since flown to Australia and living the life there. We are so heartened to see our Singapore Specials going places !
Suzuki in Houston

​Stunning Suzuki says hi all the way from Houston!
Suzuki used to be a street dog in the East who roamed a construction site. As part of our Trap Neuter Release Programme we trapped her and sterilised her. Eventually we took her in to rehome her as the work site was being demolished for redevelopment.
​
Suzuki lasted her long flight to Houston despite her separation anxiety and claustrophobic nature. She knew her best friend, Claire and her family were waiting for her in Houston we guess! Suzuki is still getting used to things so they've gotten her a GPS tracker too just to be safe. Thank you Patterson's for adopting and loving Suzuki in spite of all of her quirks!
Foxy in Jakarta

CAS doggies are all around the world!

Check out stunning Foxy with the Dahl's in Jakarta. Foxy took the longest time to find a home of her own. She was good with people she met and took her on walks but generally aloof to others. This meant she never really won over anyone who came to visit. 

The Dahl's were looking for a canine to foster and Foxy somehow won their hearts. The rest as we know is history! Foxy ended up being adopted by the Dahl's and they eventually flew her all the way to Jakarta with them when they had to move. Thank you for giving Foxy yet another beautiful Christmas!

Our Singapore Specials are indeed going places ♥
Jacky In Hong Kong

​Five years ago, when Causes for Animals - Singapore opened its doors to street dogs and community cats, one of our first rescues was Jacky.

Jacky was affectionately known as Jack Jack and used to roam Changi Airport. Like most places in Singapore, street dogs are deemed a pest and not welcomed as part of the community. We managed to get hold of Jacky after much effort and time. Jacky took a long while to open up to us but his good nature won the hearts of Aish and her husband.
​
Jacky is now living the life in Hong Kong and we cannot thank Aish and her husband enough for loving a Singapore street dog and taking him wherever their jobs take them! Thank you for adopting a street dog in need ♥
Marshmallow In Thailand

Look who's now a new canine citizen of Thailand?

Marshmallow was adopted from us last year. He was previously known as Tucker. He has since moved with his amazing family to their new home in Thailand. We are very grateful to San and her husband for taking Marshmallow with them to Thailand and treating him as part of their lovely family.

Thank you for adopting folks and bringing Marshmallow along with your family. He is HOME!​
Mubba In The UK

Look who's halfway round the world today? 

All the way from sunny Singapore, Mubba is now a proud canine citizen  of the United Kingdom. Mubba was a street dog born in a farm in the North. We rescued her along with two of her siblings. She found a home with Kasia and family and when it was time for them to leave Singapore, Mubba was certainly going along. 

She now enjoys a yard of her own, lots of walks in the country side and tonnes of love from her beautiful family. Thank you for giving yet another street dog a home away from our streets and a lifetime of love and happiness!
Buddy In Australia

Can you believe the way things have turned out for Buddy!

Buddy previously known as Maui was rescued off the streets from a work site in the West. Buddy later found a home with Yen Ping and husband. The couple recently decided to make a move to Australia due to a job posting. Yen Ping and her husband knew they wanted Buddy to enjoy the rest of his life with them and there was no way they would leave him behind.

With the support of Causes for Animals - Singapore and Canines Matter where he stayed till he was ready to fly, Buddy is finally HOME with his mum and dad!
​
This is responsible pet ownership. A Pet is for Life. Thank you for reminding us the world still has a lot of good left in it!
​Wrinkle In The USA
​
Here's a beautiful update all the way from America!
Wrinkle Boo Boo as we affectionately called him was rescued from a construction site in the East. We found him with two of his brothers who have also found homes to call their own.
​
Wrinkle was adopted by Jennifer and family a few years back. When they moved back to America, they took Wrinkle all the way home with them!
Wrinkle is enjoying his first Winter and he is loving it! We would like to thank Jennifer and her family for taking a Singapore Special with them all the way from Sunny Singapore to America. We hope that every street dog will one day find love and comfort with families to call their own!
​Jerry In The USA

​Jerry was adopted from CAS three years ago by Neil and his wife! He was renamed Moscow.

Moscow recently left sunny Singapore for America in early 2016! He now has his own yard to play in, has experienced snow and loves his romps outdoors with his mummy!

Moscow was rescued from a family who barely fed him and he came to us malnourished and fearful. He loved his brother Ben and the duo gradually came out of their shells over time. Moscow eventually won the hearts of Neil and his wife. We would like to thank them for taking Jerry home to America and for giving a Singapore Special an amazing home to call its own! 
Charcoal In London

Look who is living it up all the way in London!

Baby Charcoal was found in a sand pile with a huge wound on her back last year. The wound was filled with maggots and she was covered in ticks. She literally had been drained of blood. She was tiny and we had little hope she would survive. We did all we could to help Charcoal feel better for we were worried each day was her last.

Thankfully, the wonderful folks at The Animal Doctors, Singapore helped with the daily bandage changing, wound cleaning and ensuring Charcoal's wound healed. Charcoal blossomed into a beautiful soul with a loving personality and a never say die attitude.

Charcoal is now happily adopted by Sari and family who have since flew her to London with them in late 2015. Though her physical scars remain, we know Charcoal is loved and cherished by her amazing family. We are happy to see yet another Singapore Special in another part of the world with a second chance at life!
Wrinkle In California

A boy and his dog. The best thing in the world!

Wrinkle Boo Boo as we affectionately called him was born to a construction site in the East. We took Wrinkle and his brothers in and the trio were lucky enough to find beautiful homes to call their own.

Wrinkle left Singapore in 2015 with his family and is now living it up in California. A Singapore Special, born to our streets and now a canine resident of sunny California! Look at him running with Devon, his best pal!

We would like to say a special thank you to Jennifer and family for loving Wrinkle and bringing him all the way to California with them. We are sure Wrinkle has nothing but love and loyalty for his wonderful family!
